About This App

This tool provides access to 4-second SCADA data published by the Australian Energy Market Operator (AEMO) via the NEMWEB FPPDAILY report. Data is available from 9 December 2024 when the Frequency Performance Payments (FPP) scheme commenced non-financial operation.

Data is fetched live from AEMO on each request — no data is stored on this server. Maximum request window: 1 day per query.

SCADA data: AEMO NEMWEB FPPDAILY  |  BESS list: AEMO NEM Generation Information  |  Measurement scheme: AEMO Frequency Performance Payments (FPP)  |  Not affiliated with AEMO.
